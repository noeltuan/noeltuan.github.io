Akashic Records Consultation

An Akashic Records Consultation can be done face to face or via an online call. This is a question and answer dialogue session that takes 1 to 1.5 hours. This is sacred time, so plan your day so that you are not rushed. The consultation costs $160 USD.

You can bring something to record any information you receive. This is very useful for you to look back over the information you receive. As I only act as a channel to pass the information, I will not remember anything that was communicated to you while I am in your records. You are responsible for who you choose to share the information you receive.

If you are ready for a consultation, please contact me to arrange a date and time.

Tarot Reading via the Akashic Records

Via the Akashic Records, I have learnt to do Tarot Readings through the Osho Zen Tarot cards. These tarot readings are meant to give insight and clarity into any issue. The issue can be and is not limited to relationships, career, money, spirituality or simply wanting more awareness about the present.

The session is done remotely without you being present. Once being presented your query, I will designate a date and time to access your Akashic Records. Before your session, give yourself a moment of quietness. From this space of quietness, drop your attention to your heart and grant me permission to access your Akashic Records so that I can access the relevant information meant for your highest good. Please kindly inform me when permission is granted otherwise I will not be able to proceed with your session.

Should you require more clarity after receiving the report, we can then arrange for a separate session for a quick dialogue.

The Tarot Reading costs US$ 55 per card. Depending on your topic of interest and the spread required for clarity, the number of cards used can range from 1 to 5. The number of cards used will be discussed and agreed upon prior to the Tarot Reading.

If you are ready for a tarot reading, please contact me to arrange a date and time.
