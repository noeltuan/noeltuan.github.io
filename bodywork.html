Akashic Bodywork

Through working with the Akashic Records, I was guided to delve into Breathwork, Tantric and Tao practices for my personal healing of sexual dysfunction and trauma, to deepen my exploration of intimacy and sexuality and to release stagnant emotions. Through my self-healing I was guided by the Akashic Records to act as their physical proxy. To learn Breathwork, Tantric and Tao modalities to assist others in their rediscovery of their True Self. Akashic Bodywork was born.
