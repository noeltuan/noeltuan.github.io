Akashic Bodywork

Through working with the Akashic Records, I was guided to delve into Breathwork, Tantric and Tao practices for my personal healing of sexual dysfunction and trauma, to deepen my exploration of intimacy and sexuality and to release stagnant emotions. My healing was profound! No more sexual dysfunction! Emotions and trauma were released and I found deep intimacy and sexuality in my relationship. I felt alive, liberated and open!

Through my self-healing I was guided by the Akashic Records to act as their physical proxy. To learn Breathwork, Tantric and Tao modalities to assist others in their rediscovery of their True Self. Akashic Bodywork was born.

Akashic Bodywork is a combination of Breath, Emotion, Meditation, Movement, Sound and Touch, guided with the Akashic Records. Akashic Bodywork empowers your body’s natural process for healing, enhancing personal and spiritual growth, releasing chronic tension and restructuring your systems on a cellular level. The aim is for you to once again feel the full range of emotions and sensations you are capable of as a human being and celebrate life to the fullest.
