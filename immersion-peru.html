Inner Illuminations

September 15 - 25, 2020, Peru


An Akashic Records Medicine Retreat

“Nature itself journeyed, seeking illumination.”
- Paulo Coelho

Are you ready to be the change that changes your being? Are you ready for a life changing experience?

Nestled in the stunning foothills of the Andean mountains in Peru, let the forces of nature and ancestral spirit illuminate your magic and uncover your purpose.

This is an invitation to discover who you are beyond your persona, to tap into your own wisdom, and come home to your authentic self. You will be guided through an unforgettable 10 day transformational journey of exploration, healing, embodiment, and celebration.

Daily workshops will explore the basic elements of shamanism, heart-based tantra and conscious communication. You will learn to access the Akashic Records of yourself and others; as well as receive Guidance on how to navigate within the Akashic Fields. Classes will meet your level regardless if you are a beginner or an advanced practitioner.

Your stay will include 2 Ayahuasca ceremonies with an indigenous shaman and healer, who is experienced in holding a safe and traditional ceremonial space. We will be integrating the wisdom and power of these Medicines through the practices of yoga, movement, mantra and meditation. A visit to the sacred site of Machu Picchu with the wisdom of the Akashic Records will give you the opportunity to reflect amongst the breath-taking views of the sacred valley.

  • Visit Machu Picchu

What will you learn?

During the Retreat, mantras, meditation and various styles of yoga that include Hatha, Kundalini, Vinyasa and Yin will be taught. By the end of the course, everybody will be taught the theories and techniques to proficiently access their own Akashic Records and others. These are the standard materials. Possible additional layers of concepts, information, tools and wisdom varies as each Retreat is individually crafted based on the participants present and what is guided by the Medicine and Masters, Teachers and Loved Ones of the Akashic Records. Who Will Be Teaching You?

Calista Goh

Calista is an international Akashic Records consultant, teacher and healer. Born into a maternal lineage of medicine women, psychics and intuitives, Calista was exposed from a young age to oracle readings, soul mid-wifery and death.

She grew up in an environment where violence, abuse and isolation were her norm. Having experienced starvation, poverty, loss, grief and PTSD for the most part of her 20s has imbued in Calista a deep respect for integrity, empathy and compassion, igniting in her a passion (with personal experience) in the healing arts, therapy work and philanthropic activities. Calista was formerly intiated into Service after several near-death and real-death experiences throughout her late teens to 30s. She returns each time from the “Other Side” to share deeper Wisdom, fuller Love and expanded Joy to everyone she is blessed and honoured to meet. She travels around the world offering her services in Asia, UK, Europe, Morocco, Guatemala and USA. Her clients include celebrities and high-powered executives.

Calista has trained with esteemed Tibetan/Himalayan Masters (H.H Dalai Lama, H.H. Gyalwang Drukpa, H.E. Ayang Rinpoche), entheogens/psychedelics, a dying pigeon, her Navajo and Mayan ancestors, Nature, the Elementals and Hermeticism. Calista’s methods include a mix of psychotherapy, Akashic Records, shamanic techniques, yoga, dance, psychic channelling, crystal healing, sound, vibrational medicine, food and nutrition, breathwork and more. Calista is also a trained Cacao Ceremony Facilitator and a budding astrologer.

She believes Love is the strongest Medicine and shares herself fully in that sense. An artist, dancer, writer and designer at heart, Calista is also legally trained, a psychotherapist and serial entrepreneur. Her partner and her are passionate about permaculture, intentional communities and reconnecting humans back with Nature through experiences and trips.

She is currently in training with maestros in the use and wisdom of Master Plants and entheogens. Mapacho (Sacred Tobacco) and Ayahuasca are dear to her heart.

Claire Eli

Claire Eli is a movement, dance, yoga and meditation teacher who loves to explore the world through the body.

After experiencing the aftermath of sexual assault in her early teens, her body was numb. Logically she understood the impact of trauma, but she did not realise the extent to which it had crept into every part of her life. Over the past decade, yoga, dance, and meditation has allowed Claire to change her path, express herself, confront fears, and experience a sense of freedom that she always knew was there but did not know how to reach.

Claire’s journey has sparked a fascination with movement and self-discovery sending her around the globe. She has been fortunate enough to learn from many amazing teachers and mentors; teach in top studios, community centres, and health & wellness retreats; work with elite athletes, corporations, and people from all walks of life. Her experience has taken her to the UK, New York, South East Asia, Central America and Australasia. Initially studying in Greece with the US based school SYI School Yoga Institute, Claire went on to train between Spain and London with Frog Lotus Yoga and has taken additional trainings in Forrest Yoga and Tantra with The International School of Temple Arts. In 2018 Claire completed the International Study program at Peridance Capezio Dance School in New York and is currently completing an apprenticeship in the Rythmetrics Method, (mixed martial arts and dance), with her teacher Sekou Heru in New York.

She has partaken in S.N. Goenka Vipassana and has studied Buddhist mindfulness meditation techniques from teachers across India and Cambodia. Furthermore, she has gained valuable insight from working with shamanic practices and plant medicines in both the UK, Spain and Guatemala. Claire is passionate about connecting students to the wisdom held in their own body and encourages people to move from a place of inner strength, ease and curiosity.

Her classes are inclusive and she welcomes those of all levels to join in. Claire is currently based between New York and Spain.

Visit claireeli.com to find out more about Claire.

Where will the retreat be held?

Nestled in an oasis of native plants, fruit trees and a magnificent waterfall, Munay Sonqo is a place to connect to the incredible depth and beauty of all that nature offers.

The Sacred Valley of Peru, where the retreat is located, is a majestic spot in the Andes Mountains and home of the twelve mountains known as “Apus” — “luminous beings” who are said to intercede on behalf of people. Those who come to Munay Sonqo have the opportunity to commune directly with these mountains, as well as with the local curanderos and pacos (healers), who engage with us for ritual and ceremony. Munay Sonqo believes our world today benefits greatly from contact with these ancient sites, cultures and ways.

Munay Sonqo has programs ranging from yoga and other practices, to personal healing and reflection and practical workshops on Permaculture.

Guests at Munay Sonqo Retreat and Yoga Center are served delicious meals with locally-grown and lovingly prepared ingredients.

Each guest room is a reflection of the Peruvian culture of the Sacred Valley. Thanks to their staff, the entire center embodies the kindness, love and wholeness that permeates this magical place.

For more information visit Munay Songo’s website.
